About Us

Exo-Tech Society is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting STEM and technology to youths of all ages. Our work aims to bridge the gap between todays youth and tomorrows technology.

Welcome to Exo-Tech Society, a non-profit organization with a compelling mission - to inspire, educate, and empower the youth through STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) and advanced technology. Our commitment is to bridge the gap between today's youth and tomorrow's technology, fostering a new generation of innovators, thinkers, and problem solvers.

Our vision for the future

In a rapidly evolving world, understanding and harnessing the power of technology is more crucial than ever. At Exo-Tech Society, ourmission is to empower every young mind with the potential to be a catalyst for change, and through our various programs and initiatives, we aim to provide them with the tools, knowledge, and opportunities they need to excel in the fields of STEM.

Exo-Tech Society offers a variety of programs available to youth, including online classes focused on exploring coding fundamentals, hands on experience through robotics, and public outreach through a developing wikibase.

Inspiration Through Innovation

We firmly believe that innovation thrives in an environment that encourages curiosity, exploration, and collaboration. Exo-Tech Society strives to be a catalyst for such an environment, fostering a sense of wonder and discovery that can lead to groundbreaking technological advancements.

All youths are encouraged to connect with Exo-Tech Society organization members for mentorship on projects and more.
